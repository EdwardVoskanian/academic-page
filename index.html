Research Interests:

Can you imagine patterns that almost repeat but never quite do? Quasicrystals are materials that exhibit this phenomenon. Inspired by them, my work explores quasiperiodic structures arising from the roots of nonlattice Dirichlet polynomials.
I also enjoy mentoring students through guided studies and projects in fractal geometry, as well as in multidimensional continued fraction algorithms—like the LLL Algorithm—and the long-standing problem of simultaneous Diophantine approximation.
